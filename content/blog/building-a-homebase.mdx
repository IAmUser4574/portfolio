## Intent for this site

I set out to make neither the best nor the most portfolio site - there are plenty of [great](https://bruno-simon.com/) 
[examples](https://www.shiyunlu.com/) to [check](https://www.bettinasosa.com/) [out](https://sumanthsamala.com) for that.

I wanted a place I'd enjoy collecting and depositing my bits of progress as I tinker, develop, and opine on the engineering world; 
something simple, approachable, and readable for visitors.

## Design choices

Base architecture: I chose nextJs/react for the framework as I quite like the flexibility in design choice (static vs ssr components)
 and the ubiquity within the web dev community (components galore).

Deployment: Cloudflare Pages with a Github Apps integration - so far I'm getting that legendary fast, cheap, and good trifecta. 
If the site grows in scope I may have to convert from static page hosting to a containerized solution. 

## Going Forward

Changes: I plan to add a few more features to the site from my YouTrack kanban board; more on that later.

Content/Cadence: The goal is to not make this abandonware. Hopefully I can blog at a sustainable pace as well!
